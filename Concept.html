Crafting a Vision

Creating an unforgettable escape room experience starts with an enticing storyline that captivates players from the moment they step inside! Here are some key considerations to keep in mind:

Accordion Menu

Determine the age group you want to attract. Will your escape room cater to kids, adults, or all ages? Understanding your audience helps you refine your theme ideas to resonate with your players.

Evaluate the available space for your escape room. This will guide you in selecting appropriate puzzles and props, as well as determining the maximum number of players that can participate.

Explore popular genres—like mystery, history, science fiction, or pop culture—and infuse them with your unique twist. Consider how you can incorporate specific settings, scenarios, or characters that align with your audience and available space.

An engaging theme is vital, but it must be supported by visually captivating puzzles and props. Ensure that your designs enhance the overall experience and fit seamlessly into the narrative.
